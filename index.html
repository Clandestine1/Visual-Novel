Boss Shauna
When you joined Binary Assembly you had no idea how many all-nighters you'd pull to finish the massive amounts of work. Tonight was different, only because it was the day before you present your final project. You are debating about staying at Binary Assembly until after closing time to perfect your project.
You hesitate as you think about the rumors you've heard during your time here. You've heard many rumors ranging from alien abduction to a crazed former student that stalks the halls at night. The point is- no one stays after closing. Tonight you're...
You dread the long train ride home. The walk to the train station is eerily quiet. The always bustling 6 train station is deserted. You feel like you are being watched as you step onto the train platform. You turn around and see no one yet can't shake that awful feeling.
The train is empty, you breathe a sigh of relief. Just before the doors close at the next stop, an enigmatic man boards the train. You can't see his face, but you can see his clothes are stained with an unknown substance. A repugnent stench fills the air, making you cover your nose before you gag.
